DDRi portfolio potential mechanisms:
(M6620(VX-970), M4344(VX-803), M3541, M3814)

Genomic instability: a hallmark of late-stage cancers1,2 –
rationale for DDR inhibition

Key enzymes involved in the repair of DNA damage3:

  • ATM
  • ATR
  • DNA-PK

DNA-PK is thought to be induced by exposure to cancer therapeutics.
As an effector kinase, DNA-PK is the integral component of the NHEJ process for DNA DSB repair

ATR and ATM signal DNA repair by homologous recombination4

  • ATR is thought to be activated in response to DNA damage, such as double-strand breaks and replication stress5
  • ATM is thought to be activated by a single-stranded DNA-containing structure5

In cancer, loss of ATM-p53 signaling is common and leads to dependence of tumor cells on ATR to survive DNA damage6,7

Regulatory approval is dependent on the completion of the study programs and review by local regulatory authorities, and varies from country to country.
