Abituzumab
(pan-αv integrin inhibiting mAb)

Proposed profile

  • Abituzumab (EMD 525797) is a humanized IgG2 mAb designed to act against αv
    integrins1
    ─ Designed to bind to the extracellular domain of αv integrins and perturbs their cellular interactions2,3
  • A first-in-man, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study in healthy
    volunteers reported no safety concerns and abituzumab was well tolerated1
  • After phase I studies demonstrating a favorable safety profile,4,5 randomized phase II trials in prostate cancer and CRC were initiated

Phase II studies:

  • PERSEUS: monotherapy in 1L mCRPC (NCT01360840)
  • AMELION: abituzumab with cetuximab and FOLFIRI in 1L RAS wildtype, left-sided mCRC with high αvβ6 expression (NCT03688230)
*Abituzumab (EMD 525797) is an investigational agent and is not approved by Regulatory Authorities in any jurisdiction for any use.
